# Executive Summary — Budget Insights

The overall financial performance of **Zentra Dynamics** during the analyzed period was solid. The company spent **$21,632 less than budgeted**, representing a **positive variance of 1.33%**.

At a high level, this indicates that the organization maintained effective control over its budget execution.

---

## Spending by Category
Only three expense categories exceeded their allocated budgets:

- **Software:** -3.51% variance  
- **Repairs:** -2.79% variance  
- **Training:** -4.29% variance  

All remaining categories stayed within budget. Monitoring these three areas more closely in future periods could help prevent recurring overspending.

---

## Performance by Region
The regions with the strongest budget adherence were:

- **Montreal:** $11,396 under budget  
- **Toronto:** $10,020 under budget  

These regions contributed significantly to the company’s overall budget efficiency.

---

## Department-Level Insights
Among departments, the top performers were:

- **IT:** 2.98% under budget  
- **Marketing:** 2.19% under budget  

Notably, **Marketing** managed two of the top three most expensive categories (**Digital Tools** and **Events**) while remaining under budget, highlighting strong operational planning and cost control.

---

## Monthly Budget Trends
Month-to-month variance analysis revealed some inconsistencies:

- **January** showed the strongest performance with a **+4.29% positive variance**
- **February (-2.30%)** and **July (-0.75%)** experienced notable overspending
- **May (-0.04%)** and **December (-0.11%)** were very close to budget, with minimal deviations

These fluctuations may indicate **seasonal or project-based spending patterns** that could benefit from more detailed forecasting and monitoring in future periods.
